UAV/UAS Inspection and checklist when powered off

Firmware consistency OK Replaced Date Checked by
Connect the remote controller to the internet, and then launch the app. Make sure that the app and the firmware versions of the aircraft, remote controller, payloads and batteries match. Otherwise, the aircraft may not launch or risks may occur.
Insert all Intelligent Flight Batteries into the aircraft to make sure that all of their firmware verions are up to date.

Motors OK Replaced Date Checked by
Rotate the motors manually to check if they are firmly installed!
Listen for noise.
No gap between motors and base.
Mounted propellers are mounted correctly.
CCW propellers should be on motor 1 and 3.
CW propellers should be on motor 2 and 4.
Batteries OK Replaced Date Checked by
No foreign object in the battery port.
The port is not misshapen.
Battery locker is secure and will not come loose.
No visible damage on battery.
Antennas OK Replaced Date Checked by
All the antennas on the aircraft and remote controller are tightened firmly and free of damage.
Antennas are free of contact with the rotating propellers.

Gimbal (camera) OK Replaced Date Checked by
The gimbal appears intact and can rotate smoothly in all the three axes.
The lens in clean.
Dampeners have no crack or split.
Anti-drop rope is not damaged and secure.
Gimbal connector is secure.
Remote Controller OK Replaced Date Checked by
Control sticks are centered in neutral position.
No foreign object (E.g., sand or soil)
Full ranges of motion in all channels.
